Joe's Steam Rice Rolls

Rice rolls are a very popular Cantonese dish during dim sum. There are many different variations of rice rolls and many ingredients can be put inside. One of my favorite rice rolls is with char siu, which is basically barbecue pork. I love how they seem like they are not very filling, but they actually are. I can usually eat a lot at a time because the outside is so smooth.

Joe's Steam Rice Roll is located at 136-21 Roosevelt Ave, Flushing, NY 11354. Here is the link if you would like to try some!

New World Mall Food Court

I have heard many people recommend this food court as a place to eat in Flushing because there are so many different options. Personally, I have only gone to meet up with a couple of friends and I have noticed many different restaurants. I have seen some boba shops inside the food court, but I have also seen many different styles of Asian cuisine. I think that this is definitely a good spot to go to if you aren't sure what to eat and just want something quick and easy. Since there are so many possibilities inside, I did not add any photos.

The New World Mall Food Court is located at 136-20 Roosevelt Ave, Flushing, NY 11354. If you would like to see what places are inside, here is the link to it.
