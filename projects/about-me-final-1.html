About Me


Hi! My name is Britney Wong and I am currently a sophomore at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, majoring in Advertising (digital) with a minor in Information Management and Technology at the iSchool. I am graduating a semester earlier than the rest of my class, which would be Fall 2021. After graduation, I hope to work for an advertising agency, primarily in a big city, like New York City or maybe even somewhere abroad. Some fun facts about me are... I am in several different organizations on campus. I am a charter class member of an Asian-interest sorority, alpha Kappa Delta Phi. I am also in several other organizations like Asian Students in America and TNH, which is a student run ad agency here at Syracuse. I work at a boba shop on Marshall Street, called Hi Tea. Come stop by! I have a really big sweet tooth and I love to drink boba. If you see me, I'm most likely going to ask to get some boba.
