Restaurants in Brooklyn


99 Favor Taste

99 Favor Taste is a very popular hot pot place because it also offers barbecue. Many Asian families love both hot pot and barbecue, so this is the perfect place that offers both. There is a lot of variety in the food options, from many meats to lots of vegetables. I love eating hot pot here because evereyone has their own pot, so you don't have to worry about getting sick if you go with friends who are feeling unwell. It is also much more sanitary eating from your own pot, but you can still be in the same area with your friends or family.

99 Favor Taste is located at 732 61st St, Brooklyn, NY 11220. Here is the link to Google Maps if you would like to go and try it out yourself!

Avocaderia

Avocaderia is definitely a place that I want to visit when I have the chance. I have never been able to because I haven't been able to go to Brooklyn when I go to New York City. However, this is a place that serves a variety of dishes that all use avocado. If you love avocado toast, this will probably be one of the best that you will ever taste. I personally want to try their avocado toast and see if it is different than the ones that I make at home. I also would like to try one of their avocado bowls because I love eating avocado with my salad.

Avocaderia is located at 238 36th St, Brooklyn, NY 11232. If you love avocados, you would love it here!

White Tiger

This is a restaurant that serves what they call, modern Korean food. They try to source locally and use as much organic, antibiotic, and horome free, and humanely raised meats and veggies in their dishes. They also use Gluten free tamari, so they are very accomdating to people who have allergies or are more health conscious. I personally want to try this because I love eating Korean food and I always love to try new restaurants.

White Tiger is located at 601 Vanderbilt Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11238. Click here to see where it is!
